Hi there, I am Denys, CEO of WAW (Web Art Work Agency), and freelancer at UpWork.

There are many paths that you can follow in the IT sphere, one of them is the way of a freelancer which I followed and never regretted it. Compared to office work, here you are free to plan your life as you prefer it, work on hours which suit you. There always will be cons and pros to each work, on freelance I would say stability is not that easy to earn.

If you want to follow my steps, there is an option for 3 different paths:
  • Developer, who in fact creates websites, apps, or games, and his responsibility is to make things work. There are front-end developers who handle user experience and commonly use Angular, React, or Vue tech. Back-end developers write REST API for the front-end and there are a lot of popular technologies like Node or Python.
  • Designer’s role is to make things beautiful, very wide scope of work. Some artists do illustration work, making logos or illustrations. Other people do mocks for startups or websites. More complex could be CSS, which is simple coding but usually tough for creative people. Finally, there is 3D modeling which is more needed and paid work.
  • Agent who is usually a social and communicative person with a lot of responsibilities outside of the development process. English is highly required. Testing Developer and Design work, providing feedback on it. Plan startups, write tasks, verify tasks, and discuss all that with clients. Write translations, and content, research content.

The pros of joining our UpWork agency are that you will gain a home on the freelance path. Without agency it is difficult to get approved by UpWork on sign, then you have to pass the barrier of finding your first client and get your first job successfully. With an agency all that is way easier. Initially, you get a manager who will be responsible for your work, then when you will be looking for the first contract you will earn badges directly and clients will look at your profile differently. Finally, you will always have the easy go-out way, because in the agency there always will be someone who wants to get your spot. Having a mentor is also a big plus because your experience will rise way quicker, your English will be improved with the help of our teachers, and you will earn workflows based on our Experience. Your profile and CV will be modified by our experts, also you will go through a soft skills course.
